# FitWiz Video Ad Prompts

> **Goal:** Apple-quality product videos for Reels & TikTok  
> **Tool:** Seedance 2.0 (Image to Video)  
> **Settings:** 9:16 | 1080p | 10s | 1x speed  

---

## Key Rules for Sharp App Footage

- **Never let the AI generate the screen content** — it will always be blurry/hallucinated
- **Film real screen recordings separately** → composite them onto the phone in post (CapCut/After Effects)
- **Use the AI video ONLY for the human + environment + phone-as-prop** (screen dark or solid color)
- Apple does this exact technique — the phone screen is ALWAYS replaced in post-production

---

## Prompt Template Structure

```
[SUBJECT] + [ACTION WITH PHONE] + [ENVIRONMENT] + [LIGHTING] + [CAMERA STYLE] + [MOOD]
```

## REELS PROMPTS (Premium, Clean, Aspirational)

### R1 — Gym Morning Routine
> Cinematic shot of a fit woman in a modern gym at golden hour. She holds an iPhone in her left hand, screen facing camera showing a solid bright teal glow. She taps the screen with her right thumb and smiles with quiet confidence. Shallow depth of field, background gym equipment softly blurred. Warm amber light streams through floor-to-ceiling windows. Camera slowly tracks from her hand up to her face. Shot on anamorphic lens, 9:16 vertical, premium Apple product commercial aesthetic. Photorealistic, no text on screen.

### R2 — Kitchen Nutrition Check
> Close-up of hands holding an iPhone over a kitchen counter with fresh meal prep ingredients — chicken, rice, colorful vegetables. The phone screen shows a solid warm gradient glow. One hand taps the screen. Camera slowly pulls back to reveal a young man in a clean modern kitchen, natural daylight from a window behind him. He looks satisfied and begins plating food. Cinematic color grade, shallow focus, vertical 9:16, Apple-style product lifestyle shot. Photorealistic skin detail, no text overlays.

### R3 — Evening Wind-Down
> A woman sits on a yoga mat in a minimalist apartment at dusk. Soft warm lamp light. She holds her iPhone at a comfortable angle, screen showing a solid purple glow. She scrolls with her thumb, then sets the phone down beside her and begins stretching. Camera is low angle, slowly drifting. Cozy, aspirational, premium feel. Anamorphic bokeh, vertical 9:16. Apple commercial quality. Photorealistic.

### R4 — Morning Run Check
> A man stands at his front door in running gear, morning blue light. He lifts his iPhone, screen shows a solid orange glow. He glances at the screen, nods, puts in earbuds and steps outside into soft sunrise light. Camera follows him from inside through the doorway. Shallow depth of field, golden hour rim lighting on his silhouette. Cinematic, aspirational, vertical 9:16. Photorealistic, Apple fitness ad aesthetic.

## POST-PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

### Step 1: Screen Recording
1. Screen-record the actual FitWiz app flows on a real iPhone
2. Key flows to capture:
   - Workout plan scrolling (3-4s)
   - AI coach chat exchange (3-4s)  
   - Nutrition log / meal photo (3-4s)
   - Exercise details with timer (3-4s)
   - Progress chart / XP animation (3-4s)

### Step 2: AI Video Generation
1. Generate the human + environment video using prompts above
2. The phone screen will be a solid color / glow (intentional)

### Step 3: Screen Composite (CapCut / After Effects)
1. Track the phone screen in the AI video (corner pin / motion track)
2. Replace the solid glow with your real screen recording
3. Match the screen brightness to the scene lighting
4. Add subtle screen reflections for realism

### Step 4: Text Overlays

| Timing | Reels | TikTok |
|--------|-------|--------|
| 0-1.5s | "What if your phone coached every rep?" | "POV: you downloaded a personal trainer" |
| 3-5s | "Workouts. Meals. AI Coach." | "It literally plans everything for you" |
| 7-10s | "Try FitWiz free — link in bio" | "FitWiz. Link in bio." |

### Step 5: Audio
- **Reels:** Clean lo-fi beat or ambient gym sounds
- **TikTok:** Trending sound (check TikTok Creative Center for current trends)

## NOTES

- Always use **1080p** minimum — 720p will look soft on modern phones
- If Seedance only offers 720p, upscale with Topaz Video AI before compositing
- The **solid color screen trick** is non-negotiable — AI cannot render readable UI
- Test each prompt 2-3 times, cherry-pick the best generation
- Vertical video (9:16) for all social — never 16:9
