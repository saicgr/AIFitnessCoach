3. AI-Generated Content and Services

Important: FitWiz uses Google Gemini artificial intelligence to generate workout plans, nutrition advice, coaching responses, and exercise recommendations. You should be aware of the following:

  • AI Processing: Your fitness profile, health data, workout history, and chat messages are sent to Google Gemini AI to generate personalized responses. This data is transmitted securely to Google's servers for processing.
  • Not Medical Advice: All AI-generated content is for informational and educational purposes only. It does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before starting any exercise or nutrition program.
  • Content Accuracy: While we strive for accuracy, AI-generated workout and nutrition content may contain errors. We recommend verifying exercise form with qualified trainers and consulting healthcare providers for health-related decisions.
  • RAG System: We use a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) system with an exercise and workout database to improve the relevance and accuracy of AI recommendations. Your queries are matched against our curated exercise database to provide better suggestions.
  • Multi-Agent System: Our AI coaching uses specialized agents for different domains (workout planning, nutrition, injury prevention, hydration) to provide comprehensive and contextual advice.

4. Health Connect and HealthKit Integration

FitWiz integrates with Android Health Connect and Apple HealthKit to read and write health and fitness data. This integration is optional and requires your explicit permission.

Data We Read

  • Weight, height, body fat percentage, lean body mass, body water mass, bone mass
  • Heart rate, resting heart rate, heart rate variability
  • Steps, distance, active and total calories burned
  • Oxygen saturation, blood pressure
  • Sleep data
  • Exercise sessions
  • Basal metabolic rate

Data We Write

  • Weight measurements
  • Height measurements
  • Body fat measurements
  • Exercise sessions completed in FitWiz

Health Connect/HealthKit Data Policy: Health data accessed through Health Connect or HealthKit is used solely for the purpose of providing fitness coaching and tracking within the FitWiz app. This data is never sold to third parties, used for advertising, or shared outside the services necessary to provide app functionality. You can revoke Health Connect or HealthKit permissions at any time through your device settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Specific retention periods:

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account.
  • Workout and Fitness Data: Retained until you delete your account or request deletion of specific data.
  • Chat History: Retained for up to 12 months to provide continuity in AI coaching sessions, then automatically archived.
  • Health Connect / HealthKit Data: Synced data is processed in real-time and cached locally on your device. Server-side health data is retained only as long as your account is active.
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized analytics may be retained indefinitely for service improvement.

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses TLS/HTTPS encryption.
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive data is encrypted at rest in our database systems.
  • Authentication: JWT-based authentication with automatic token refresh and session management.
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access controls limit who can access user data.
  • Secure Storage: Sensitive credentials on your device are stored using platform-secure storage (Keychain on iOS, EncryptedSharedPreferences on Android).

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your GDPR Rights

  • Right to Know: You may request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: You may request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell your personal information. If this changes, we will provide a "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

Categories of Personal Information Collected

  • Identifiers (name, email, device IDs)
  • Health and fitness information (workout data, body measurements, health metrics)

To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at privacy@fitwiz.app or use the in-app account deletion feature. We will verify your identity before processing requests. You may designate an authorized agent to make requests on your behalf.
