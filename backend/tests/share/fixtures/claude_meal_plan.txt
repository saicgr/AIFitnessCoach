Here's a 7-day high-protein meal plan targeting 2,200 calories and 180g of protein per day.

**Day 1**
- Breakfast: 4 scrambled eggs, 2 slices whole-wheat toast, 1 cup blueberries
- Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, quinoa, chickpeas, olive oil + lemon
- Snack: Greek yogurt with honey and almonds
- Dinner: 6 oz baked salmon, 1 cup brown rice, roasted broccoli

**Day 2**
- Breakfast: Protein oatmeal (1 scoop whey + oats + banana)
- Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap on a whole-wheat tortilla
- Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple
- Dinner: Lean beef stir-fry with bell peppers, snap peas, and brown rice

**Day 3**
- Breakfast: Greek yogurt parfait with granola and strawberries
- Lunch: Quinoa power bowl with grilled chicken, kale, sweet potato, tahini dressing
- Snack: Protein shake with banana
- Dinner: Whole-grain spaghetti with turkey meat sauce, side salad

**Day 4**
- Breakfast: 3-egg veggie omelet with feta, side of fruit
- Lunch: Tuna salad on whole-grain crackers, apple
- Snack: Hard-boiled eggs and almonds
- Dinner: Grilled chicken thighs, mashed cauliflower, roasted asparagus

**Day 5**
- Breakfast: Whey protein smoothie with spinach, banana, peanut butter
- Lunch: Chicken Caesar wrap (light dressing)
- Snack: String cheese and grapes
- Dinner: Baked cod, lentil pilaf, sautéed zucchini

**Day 6**
- Breakfast: Cottage cheese with mixed berries and walnuts
- Lunch: Turkey chili (lean ground turkey + beans + tomatoes)
- Snack: Protein bar
- Dinner: Sirloin steak, baked sweet potato, green beans

**Day 7**
- Breakfast: Veggie scramble (3 eggs, spinach, mushrooms), toast
- Lunch: Buddha bowl with chickpeas, brown rice, roasted veggies, tahini
- Snack: Greek yogurt with chia seeds
- Dinner: Grilled shrimp tacos on corn tortillas, slaw, avocado

**Hydration**: Aim for 3-4 liters of water per day. Adjust portions up or down by 10% based on whether your weight is moving as expected after the first 2 weeks.
