Sure! Here's a 4-day Upper/Lower workout split designed for intermediate lifters focused on hypertrophy:

**Day 1 — Upper Body (Push focus)**
1. Barbell Bench Press — 4 sets of 6-8 reps, rest 2 minutes
2. Seated Overhead Press — 4 sets of 8 reps, rest 90 seconds
3. Incline Dumbbell Press — 3 sets of 10 reps
4. Cable Lateral Raises — 3 sets of 12-15 reps
5. Triceps Rope Pushdowns — 3 sets of 12 reps
6. Overhead Triceps Extensions — 3 sets of 10 reps

**Day 2 — Lower Body (Quad focus)**
1. Back Squat — 4 sets of 6 reps, rest 3 minutes
2. Bulgarian Split Squats — 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
3. Leg Press — 3 sets of 12 reps
4. Leg Extensions — 3 sets of 15 reps
5. Standing Calf Raises — 4 sets of 15 reps

**Day 3 — Upper Body (Pull focus)**
1. Weighted Pull-Ups — 4 sets of 6-8 reps, rest 2 minutes
2. Barbell Row — 4 sets of 8 reps, rest 90 seconds
3. Lat Pulldowns — 3 sets of 10 reps
4. Cable Rows — 3 sets of 12 reps
5. Barbell Curls — 3 sets of 10 reps
6. Hammer Curls — 3 sets of 12 reps

**Day 4 — Lower Body (Posterior chain)**
1. Romanian Deadlifts — 4 sets of 6 reps, rest 3 minutes
2. Hip Thrusts — 3 sets of 10 reps
3. Walking Lunges — 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
4. Seated Leg Curls — 3 sets of 12 reps
5. Standing Calf Raises — 4 sets of 15 reps

Aim for progressive overload week over week. Track your reps and weights, and add 2.5-5 lbs whenever you hit the top of the rep range with good form.
