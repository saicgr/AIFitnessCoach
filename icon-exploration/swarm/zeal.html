Zealova — App Icon Concepts

Creative territory: zeal — contained energy, drive, the inner spark that makes someone show up and push. Forbidden and avoided: literal flame, lightning bolt, block letter Z, negative-space-letter-in-a-square, dumbbells, human figures, gradients/3D/gloss.

Flat, solid colors only. Palette: #34D399 light · #10B981 primary · #059669 deep, on a near-black #080809 rounded square. Each mark is built as a reusable <symbol> and shown at display size plus 60 px and 40 px for legibility.

1 · Updraft

Energy in motion

Two thick crescent blades with flat-cut ends, the upper offset up-and-right of the lower, so the pair reads as one upward thrust. The dark channel between them is a clean rising gap; the two-step green gives flat depth. It captures the lift of zeal without a flame or a bolt, and the hard-cut edges keep it faceted and energetic rather than soft.

2 · Core Pulse

Charged nucleus

A solid hot core with three chunky arrowhead shards orbiting it, each pitched the same rotational way: a pinwheel of force, energy held at the centre and pushing outward. The lighter inner dot is the spark. Symmetrical and badge-like for instant recognition, but the directional shards keep it spinning rather than static.

60 px
40 px
On a home screen

3 · Vault

Drive in one shape

One bold solid wedge thrusting upward, with a chevron notch bitten from its base. It is multi-readable: the negative-space notch is an upward chevron and forward arrow, while the green mass reads as a body mid-leap or a peak being summited. A light facet on the right half adds flat dimension. The single confident form is the most legible of the three and survives hardest at small sizes.

60 px
40 px
On a home screen
Recommended direction: Vault — the boldest silhouette, the strongest second read, and the most reliable at 40–60 px. Updraft is the most distinctive gesture if a softer, more kinetic mark is preferred.
