Zealova — App Icon: Emblem Concepts

Chunky faceted badges in the Gravl school. Flat, solid green (#10B981 / #34D399 / #059669) on near-black #080809. Each mark uses negative space as a deliberate secondary image. Shown at hero size, then 60 px and 40 px to verify small-scale legibility.

Concept 1

Rise

A solid faceted slab cut to an upward peak, with a bold open chevron carved clean through its centre. Peak and chevron point the same way, so the whole badge pushes up in one beat.

Negative space: the chevron void leaves the lower positive mass reading as one thick upward arrowhead — the empty space and the solid both resolve to ascent.

Concept 2

Lift-Gate

A heavy faceted shield. A tall hard-edged void is carved up from the base; the two thick legs flanking it converge to one peaked apex — two disciplines driving a single ascent.

Negative space: the carved void is a clean upward arrowhead; the positive frame doubles as a gateway you move up through.

Concept 3

Keystone

A chunky hexagonal badge split by a hard seam into a crowning block and a wider base. A triangular keystone wedge locks the seam — two halves fused into one structure.

Negative space: the mirrored V-bites top and bottom carve an upward spark at the joint — the keystone reads as both a lock and a flare of zeal.
