Zealova — App Icon Exploration: Monogram Territory

Three lettermark concepts. Flat, solid green (#10B981 / #34D399 / #059669) on near-black #080809. Each abstracts the Z far enough to read as a shape first — built to dodge the Zwift trap (a plain block Z) and the negative-space-letter-in-a-square stock trope.

CONCEPT 01 Notch

One cut, two masses. A bold solid tile split by a single Z-shaped channel into two interlocking slabs — light above, deep below — slotting together like a two-piece puzzle. Reads as a split badge; the Z lives only in the near-black gap. The training+nutrition split.
