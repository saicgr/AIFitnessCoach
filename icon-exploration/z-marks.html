Five custom Z marks

Built the way Hevy, MacroFactor, Gravl and GymBeat are built: one custom letterform, flat, two colours, high contrast. Brand orange #F97316 on near-black — never orange-background, because GymBeat already owns that. None of these is a font Z dropped on a square; each is a constructed mark. My honest read — after rendering them

Speed and Knockout are the standouts. Speed has the most character — a forward lean and sliced terminals, athletic, still unmistakably a Z. Knockout has the most presence on a home screen — a solid orange block pulls the eye in the grid.

Ribbon is the clean, modern, safe pick. Bold is the dependable workhorse. Curve has the best idea — a Z in motion — but the swept diagonal renders slightly lumpy; it would need a refinement pass to sit right.

Pick one. I refine that exact mark — proportions, curve tension, corner weight, 2-3 variations — then export to PNG and wire it into the iOS AppIcon.appiconset + Android mipmap set. It ships with the next build.
