# TikTok Videos — Claude Instructions

Read `marketing/CLAUDE.md` first. TikTok is the highest-leverage platform for B2C fitness apps in 2026 — and the most punishing if you get the first 3 seconds wrong.

## TikTok 2026 reality (verify with WebSearch every session)

- **First 3 seconds = the entire video.** Watch-rate at 3s is the #1 ranking signal. Lose them by 3s, the algo dumps you.
- **Sweet spot length: 15–30 seconds**. Longer videos only work for storytelling threads or tutorials with clear cliffhangers.
- **Trending sounds are mandatory.** Original audio gets <30% of trending audio's reach.
- **Vertical 9:16 only.** 1080×1920 minimum, 60fps where possible.
- **Captions hard-coded** in video (TikTok auto-captions are inconsistent and look low-effort).
- **No CapCut watermark.** Strip it before upload — algorithm de-ranks recognizably edited videos.
- **For You Page selection** is heavily weighted on completion rate and rewatch rate. Hooks that loop ("the answer is in the last frame") drive rewatches.
- **Comment replies via video** are a free distribution boost — algo treats reply-videos as new posts with the original's audience pre-attached.

## Hook formulas (proven 2026 for fitness/tech)

1. **Curiosity gap** — "I built an AI fitness coach in 30 days as a solo dev. The hardest part wasn't the AI."
2. **Educational tease** — "3 things every fitness app gets wrong about calorie tracking. #2 is why MyFitnessPal feels broken."
3. **Result-focused** — "POV: you snap a photo of your plate and your calories are logged in 2 seconds." [show actual app]
4. **Pain + reveal** — "Stop typing 'grilled chicken, ~6 oz' into MyFitnessPal. There's a faster way." [demo]
5. **Trend hijack** — Use trending sound + on-screen text relating sound to fitness app angle.

## Script structure (15-second template)

```
[0:00–0:01]  HOOK — text on frame + audible hook word + bold visual
[0:02–0:05]  PAIN — show the broken status quo (typing into competitor app)
[0:06–0:11]  REVEAL — show the Zealova flow that fixes it
[0:12–0:14]  PAYOFF — quantified outcome ("logged in 2 seconds, ~95% accurate macros")
[0:15]       LOOP / CTA — final frame either loops back to hook OR has "follow for more" overlay
```

## Format checklist

- [ ] 9:16, 1080×1920, 30–60 fps
- [ ] Trending sound (verified trending in last 7 days)
- [ ] Hard-coded captions throughout (legible on mute)
- [ ] Hook text on frame within first second
- [ ] No watermark from editing software
- [ ] First frame is the value prop, not a logo or title card
- [ ] Total length 15–30 sec
- [ ] Description: 1–2 sentences + 3–4 hashtags

## Hashtag strategy

3–4 tags max. Mix:
- 1 broad fitness/AI tag (#fitnessapp, #aitools)
- 1 mid-niche (#fitnessjourney, #appdevelopment)
- 1 community (#buildinpublic, #indiedev for tech-curious; #gymtok for fitness)
- 1 trending tag from the chosen sound

## Caption (description) template

```
[Hook line — 5–10 words, echoes video hook]
[1 sentence value prop]
#fitnessapp #aitools #buildinpublic
```

Max 150 chars total. Anything longer gets truncated and de-prioritized.

## Anti-patterns

- Talking head with no on-screen text → mute viewers leave at 1 sec
- "Hi guys, in today's video I want to show you..." opener → instant skip
- Original audio (no trending sound) → <30% of normal reach
- 3-min videos → rare to retain, almost never picked up by FYP for new accounts
- Watermarked CapCut / TikTok export → quietly de-ranked
- Generic #fyp #foryou hashtags → ignored by algo since 2024
- Reposting from Reels → de-ranked. Always shoot/edit native.
