## What NOT to do

- Don't argue trademark validity. Their registration is final on the
  Principal register; arguing validity requires a TTAB cancellation
  proceeding which costs $50K+ and 18+ months. Argue *non-confusion*
  on facts.
- Don't claim "FITWIZ" is generic or descriptive. It's neither. The
  Principal register issuance settled that.
- Don't admit knowledge of their mark before a specific date. Stick to
  factual differentiation.
- Don't engage publicly (Twitter, Reddit, etc) about the dispute —
  every public statement becomes evidence.
- Don't use "Coach" in copy that resembles complainant's tagline.
- Don't add additional FITWIZ assets to the codebase, marketing, or
  swag while the dispute is open.
