Program Library — Signature v2 Directions

Eleven full-screen proofs for Zealova's Program Library, rebuilt in the canonical signature v2 design language — tinted near-black surfaces, Anton mastheads, Barlow Condensed labels, Fraunces serif at the warm beats, Space Mono telemetry, and orange spent exactly once per screen. Frame 00 renders today's flat chip-filtered list plus an honest critique; the ten directions that follow each propose a distinct browsing paradigm. Every frame now reads as a sub-screen pushed from the Workouts tab — back chevron + ZEALOVA/Anton header up top, the five-room bottom nav with WORKOUTS active below, and the floating + FAB. Real program names, real numbers — nothing is placeholder.

Beginner Intermediate Advanced Elite Accent / selected
★ Recommended direction

Lead with #03 Goal-First. It maps to how users actually arrive — with a goal, not a category — and one tap collapses 212 programs into a confident shortlist with live counts. It's the strongest answer to "where do I even start?"

Graft in #02 Netflix Rails (intent-titled poster rails for mood-based discovery) and #06 Coach-Curated Feed (leans on Zealova's real moat — the AI coach + your training history). #10 Category Hub is the runner-up: a clean fallback for specialized seekers browsing by domain.
