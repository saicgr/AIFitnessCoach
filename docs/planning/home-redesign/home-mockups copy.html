You're right — Zealova isn't a workout-only app. Workout and Nutrition are now co-equal pillars; which one leads is personalized to the user.

You corrected me — and you're right

I wrongly assumed everyone opens Zealova to train. For your friends, the draw is restaurant menu scan and import food from your fridge — nutrition is their #1. A fixed "START WORKOUT hero on top" is wrong for them.

The fix — two co-equal cards, personalized order

Workout and Nutrition are now two full-width cards of equal weight. Each carries its own primary actions — Workout: START; Nutrition: Scan Menu + Log Meal (fridge import sits in the ⋯ / and as a Quick Action). Neither is a small squashed square anymore.

Which card leads is personalized — three inputs, in order: (1) an onboarding question — "What brings you to Zealova: Training · Eating better · Both?"; (2) behavior — if you log food far more than you train, Nutrition auto-rises (and vice-versa); (3) manual — the existing "Edit home screen" reorder. Below: the same home, workout-led vs nutrition-led — only the order swaps, both cards are fully capable.
