# Personal Planning Docs

Operational planning for shipping Zealova + the other 3 apps while keeping a day job, gym, marriage, and job-search alive.

These are **personal docs**, not project technical docs. The rest of `/docs/` is for project reference (architecture, features, audits, etc.).

---

## Files

### [LAUNCH_SPRINT.md](./LAUNCH_SPRINT.md) — read this FIRST (May 12-25)

**Active sprint document.** 2-week iOS App Store launch sprint for Zealova. Overrides the normal weekly schedule until iOS goes live.

Day-by-day Apple submission checklist, launch day playbook (hour-by-hour), tracking sections for end-of-week reviews.

**Use this until** Zealova iOS is live on App Store (~May 22-25).

### [WEEKLY_SCHEDULE.md](./WEEKLY_SCHEDULE.md) — steady-state operational plan

The long-term weekly rhythm: sleep, day job, job apps (~825/wk), gym, side app building (vibe-coding across 4 apps), content posts (~33/wk), wife time.

Per-day tables Mon-Sun with explicit time blocks. Per-app social map (search by app name to find every social touchpoint).

**Use this from** Week 4 onward, after Zealova launches and life returns to normal rhythm.

---

## How to use both files together

```
NOW (May 12) ────────────────► May 25 ─────────────────► Week 4+
└── LAUNCH_SPRINT.md active ──┘└── WEEKLY_SCHEDULE.md resumes
```

During the sprint:
- LAUNCH_SPRINT.md overrides the build-block contents in WEEKLY_SCHEDULE.md
- Sleep / day job / gym / job apps / wife time stay the same in both
- Marketing volume drops in sprint (15 posts/wk) vs steady-state (33 posts/wk)

After launch:
- WEEKLY_SCHEDULE.md resumes
- LAUNCH_SPRINT.md becomes archive (or template for Hireable/Mobi/UV launches later)

---

## Tracking

- Update LAUNCH_SPRINT.md daily during the sprint (check off completed items, fill in metrics)
- Sunday 9:30pm weekly review block: 5-min audit of both files
- After launch: archive LAUNCH_SPRINT.md as `LAUNCH_SPRINT_ZEALOVA_2026-05.md` and replicate the template for Hireable/Mobi/UV launches
