Buddy & Bowl — built like an illustrator would

Two characters, drawn with volume (soft gradients, eye catchlights, contact shadows) and animated on real principles: idle breathing, periodic blinking, squash-and-stretch on every bite, sloshing fuel, drifting steam. No slider — tap a clearly labeled state, or 🍴 Feed to watch it eat. Open in a browser (VS Code's preview blocks the animation).

Blob Buddy idles with a slow breath and the occasional blink. Feed it and it does an anticipation-dip then a springy overshoot while the energy sloshes up inside. Hungry = pale, droopy lids, worried brows. On track = soft smile. Goal = beaming open smile, rosy cheeks, twinkles. Over = inflates wide, brows up, one sweat bead drips, mouth goes woozy and it turns peach.
Cozy Bowl always has soft steam drifting up. The food level springs as you eat, with a rounded surface + garnish. Goal plants a little flag. Over = the food heaps above the rim, a dumpling tumbles out beside the bowl, and the steam warms to orange with a gentle "!".
