What you're watching

• Idle: it breathes (squash & stretch), blinks, and bobs — never frozen.
• Feeding: the translucent belly fills with a warm food liquid that sloshes; a little hop + belly-jiggle each time you log.
• Goal: star eyes, a celebratory hop, sparkles, fill reaches the gold line.
• Over the limit: belly overflows past the max line and spills; the buddy flushes warm, cheeks puff, a sweat drop, a strained wobble — the further over, the more strained.

How it ships in Flutter

This mockup is hand-built SVG so you can judge the art/motion now. In the app it ships as a Rive state machine in hero_nutrition_card.dart: one input fullness (0–1.3) fed eaten/goal from the nutrition provider, one justAte trigger fired on each log. Rive gives illustrator-grade frames at 60fps with the exact same arc — no per-state PNGs, one vector rig.
