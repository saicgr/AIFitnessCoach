V3.1 — YOUR QUESTIONS

"HOW DOES THE SCREEN START?"

Use the STATE buttons above the phone. 1 · START is the cold open: a big "SNAP YOUR FRIDGE" zone, gallery / type-instead alternatives, a 3-step "how it works" strip, and — for returning users — a "Your last scan" resume card. Tapping snap plays 2 · SCANNING (sweep line over your photo + a live "ingredients found" counter ticking up) and lands on 3 · RESULTS automatically.

"WHAT IS REMIX THE MENU?"

Renamed to what it does: GET 5 NEW RECIPES ✦ — "same fridge, different ideas." When you've changed filters or benched ingredients it flips to the amber APPLY & REGENERATE ✦.

"ONLY 3? WHY 'TONIGHT'S MENU'?"

All 5 recipes render now, and the section is time-neutral: YOUR RECIPES · 5 found · sorted by match. (The MEAL filter — Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner/Snack — covers the time-of-day intent properly instead of the header guessing.)

"MORE FILTERS?"

Five groups now: MEAL (breakfast→snack), GOAL (+high fiber), TIME & EFFORT (≤15 / ≤30 / one-pan / no-cook), CUISINE (Mediterranean / Mexican / Asian / Indian / any), DIET (+gluten-free, pre-filled from your saved preferences).

"IMAGES FOR COOK-MODE STEPS?"

Honest answer: stock photos can't match specific steps ("add the garlic sauce to YOUR skillet") — they'd look wrong more often than right. So Cook Mode keeps the dish's hero photo pinned at the top (now in the mock) with big readable text per step. If you ever want true per-step images, Gemini image gen can do it at ~$0.04 × 4 steps = ~16¢/recipe, cacheable — viable later as a premium touch, wrong place to start.

"FILTERS MINIMIZED + RESET + ALL RECIPES?"

Collapsed by default — one row: "FILTERS · none — showing everything ▾". The default state has NO constraints (only your saved diet restrictions stay on, labeled "always applied"), so out of the box the AI generates freely = "all recipes". Open it, toggle anything, and an amber Reset appears that restores defaults. The collapsed summary always tells you what's active ("2 active · High protein, ≤30 min").

MOOD SELECTOR

New always-visible row above filters: 🍲 Comfort · 🥗 Fresh & light · 🌶️ Spicy · 😴 Lazy & quick · 🎉 Impress someone · 🍫 Sweet tooth. Single-select, optional, goes straight into the Gemini prompt ("the user is in a comfort-food mood — lean warm, hearty, one-pot"). It's the fun dial that filters can't express.

WHICH KEY DOES WHAT

Gemini does both AI jobs: Vision reads the fridge photo (ingredient detection) and the text model generates the recipes (now also fed mood + filters). Pexels only fetches photos of the finished dishes for the cards. Both keys live in Render env.
