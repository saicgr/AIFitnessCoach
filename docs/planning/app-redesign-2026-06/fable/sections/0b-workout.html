GDepth Field — the set logger as a cockpit
Z-depth does the hierarchy: orbs drift far behind, the set log recedes to a mid glass layer, the rest ring floats on its own elevation, and the focal card hovers closest with the boldest shadow — a 3D dumbbell resting right on it. Risk: glassmorphism turns to slop the moment anyone gets lazy with elevation rules — and backdrop blur on a screen that stays awake 50 minutes is a real battery tax; ship with a static-fallback mode or it's dishonest.
