HKinetic Collage — the scrapbook log
The food diary as a zine page: each meal is a white sticker rotated ±2° with the render taped on under a volt strip, mono-stamp timestamps, an Anton TODAY stamp, the 1,460 circled in orange marker, and macro totals as three semantic-color stamped chips. Risk: cute-fatigue is real at meal 4 of day 90; and once real food renders replace emoji, photographic dishes compete with the sticker chrome — the tape/rotation has to defer or it turns into noise.
