GDepth Field — spatial glass at engineered depth
The visionOS take: three drifting color orbs give the black actual depth, frosted cards float at exactly three fixed elevations, and 3D objects perch ON the glass with one consistent top-left light. Depth is a system (elevation scale + shadow ramp), not decoration — no fitness competitor ships spatial UI.
Risk: one lazy execution and it reads as 2021 glassmorphism slop. Backdrop blur is the most expensive effect on low-end Android GPUs, and the discipline (max 3 elevations, no blur behind dense text) must survive 180 screens of contributors.
