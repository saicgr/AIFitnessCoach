ZEALOVA SIGNATURE — THE APP
The chosen dark direction, built. The proposed final screens — not before/after pairs.
A · Home B · Active workout C · Nutrition D · Nav consistency
One mood, one type system: Anton mastheads and numerals, Barlow Condensed labels, Fraunces serif at the emotional beats, Space Mono telemetry, Inter body. Solid orange spent exactly once per screen; sheets are the only glass surface; macros stay semantic (protein violet, carbs cyan, fat orange). This build corrects two flagged regressions — the peek-next workout carousel is restored on Home, and the inline between-sets rest returns to both workout tiers — and adds the five-room nav-consistency proof. Every frame loop-animates over 10s.
Section A · The front page

Home leads with the AI coach — a card carrying the message and a real to-do list (the day's coach tasks with checkboxes + progress), like the current app's coach-insight prominence. Below it: the peek-next workout carousel and a compact FUEL strip. And Home scrolls — the third frame shows what's below the fold: the strength-score ring, the MOVE deck (steps / active kcal / sleep), habits, readiness and timeline. Nothing from today's home is lost.

Section C · The fuel ledger
NUTRITION — MEALS ARE THE STRUCTURE
Meals are the single source of truth — no duplicate conversational feed. Every food is filed under its meal (Breakfast / Lunch / Dinner / Snacks); the meal sections ARE the record, collapsible and expandable. A single food is a flat row, a multi-food entry is a grouped parent + children, and a menu-scan log carries a MENU SCAN badge + thumbnail — provenance (text / photo / menu scan) is preserved per entry. The "Log food, water, anything…" composer is just input: it files each log into the right meal by time, with a transient "✓ Added to Lunch" ghost — the durable record stays in the meal sections.

NUTRITION · EXPANDED1 breakfast · 2 lunch · menu-scan dinner
This answers "how does it show with 1 breakfast / 2 lunch / menu-scan dinner": every food is filed under its meal. Single foods are flat rows (Oatmeal, Greek Yogurt), a multi-food entry is one grouped parent + indented children on an orange connector (Salmon Poke Bowl + Iced Matcha under Lunch), and a menu-scan log carries a MENU SCAN badge + photo thumbnail (The Grill's 3 items under Dinner).
