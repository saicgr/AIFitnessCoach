Dot-trail score card
Solid arc = done. Dotted trail = what's left. The path you haven't walked yet.

The 5 problems — fixed

1
Labels. Each arc is named — TRAIN / FUEL / MOVE — in its own colour, with the real value beside it.
2
Score is the hero. The composite "27" is huge and dead center — the first thing you read, not a cryptic corner square.
3
Contrast. White card, full-strength orange/green/blue arcs, a clearly visible grey dot trail. Passes a glance and an accessibility check.
4
Buildable. No brush texture. A clean CustomPainter: 3 stroked arcs + a dotted-stroke circle. Animates and scales to any value.
5
On-brand. Zealova's white, orange, sans-serif — drops straight onto your home with nothing else changed.
Build refinement: the dots can taper (shrink + fade) as they trail away, the way the original does — a few lines in the painter.
