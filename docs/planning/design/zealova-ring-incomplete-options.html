My pick: A — Tinted track. Each segment's empty part is a soft tint of its own colour, so the ring is colour-coded even before you do anything, and it stays calm and instantly legible — the job of a thing you check five times a day. B is the most beautiful if you want minimal. C and D are bolder but busier. Tell me which, and I'll drop it into the score card.
