How the score is determined

It is not a Health Connect "health score" — HC only gives raw numbers. Today Score is Zealova's own deterministic calculation. 85% of it is your own data.

Train 50%  = today's workout complete?  → from your plan
Fuel  35%  = calorie + protein on target?  → from your food log
Move 15%  = steps vs goal  → from Health Connect (the only HC part)

score = round( (0.50·Train + 0.35·Fuel + 0.15·Move) × 100 )
no Health Connect → reweight to Train 59% / Fuel 41%
