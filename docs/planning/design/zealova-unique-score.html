2 · WHAT DECIDES THE NUMBER

The formula — deterministic, no sensor, no LLM

Train · 50% — today's scheduled workout, 0% until done → 100% when complete. The heaviest weight, because you're a coach first.
Fuel · 35% — adherence to your calorie + protein targets so far today, from your food log.
Move · 15% — steps vs goal, from Apple Health / Health Connect. The one commodity metric, kept small.
score = round( (0.50·Train
       + 0.35·Fuel + 0.15·Move) × 100 )
It starts low each morning and fills as you act — like Apple's rings, but the inputs are your coach's instructions, not raw biometrics. Naming is open: "Today Score", "Plan Score", "Coach Score".
