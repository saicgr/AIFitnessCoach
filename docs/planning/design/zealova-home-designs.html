Zealova v6 — tracker + coach, 3 zones
Utility → Today (coach/plan, heroed by the workout) → Track (tracker, heroed by the steps ring). With the tracker hero added, the nutrition card peeks below the fold — its header and kcal stay visible as a scroll cue. Water & fasting follow on scroll.

The honest tradeoff: adding the tracker ring is the right call for a tracker-and-coach app, but it costs ~135px. Something has to move below the fold, and nutrition is it — you see the steps ring fully, the nutrition card peeks. If you'd rather nutrition stay fully visible, the lever is the notification banner: make it truly transient (only on reward events) and on a normal day everything rises ~80px and nutrition clears the fold.
