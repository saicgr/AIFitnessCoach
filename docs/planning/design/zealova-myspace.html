What changed

The rim. Every segment is a capsule with a grey rim, so the empty part is clearly visible — you see the whole goal, not just the filled bit.
No weight %. Train's arc is just visibly the biggest — its size is the weight. No "50%" to decode.
No completion %. "89g protein to go" beats "42% done" — it's the actual thing to do next.
One number only: the score (27), with a plain state under it ("Just getting started"). That's the only number you read.

My Space — the metrics hub
A full screen, so it just scrolls — no swipe, no height jump. Lives on the "You" tab, renamed My Space.

Why this is the right call: a swipeable card with different-height pages would make the home screen reflow every swipe — exactly what you spotted. A fixed home card + a dedicated My Space screen fixes it cleanly: the home stays calm and stable, and My Space becomes the "focus" place to study your numbers. It also gives the You tab a real job — rename it My Space.
