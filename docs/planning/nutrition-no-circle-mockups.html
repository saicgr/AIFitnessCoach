My read: for a hero that's instantly readable and non-circular, #1 stacked budget bar or #4 number-first + sparkline. For something distinctive that no competitor ships, #6 dot budget (gamified, ties into your XP/streak scaffold) or #7 day timeline (tells the day's story). #8 is the power-user fallback. You can also layer: e.g. #4 hero number with #1's stacked bar underneath.
