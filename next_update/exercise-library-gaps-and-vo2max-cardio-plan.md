## Context
We have the open opportunity to be **the only strength-first AI app that also trains aerobic
fitness**. Competitor teardown: wearables estimate VO₂max but don't generate workouts; strength
apps (Fitbod/Hevy/**Gravl**) ignore HR/VO₂max; endurance apps don't do strength; recovery apps
(Whoop/Athlytic) tell you intensity but don't generate the session. **We're the only product with
all four inputs together: HR+VO₂max data → recovery signal → AI generation → coach.** Peter
Attia/longevity has made VO₂max + Zone 2 mainstream consumer demand (2024-26).

Surprise from the audit: **~80% of the plumbing already exists, unwired.** Frontend already has
`vo2max_detail_screen.dart`, `vo2max_repository.dart` (+ history/latest providers),
`heart_rate_provider.dart` (Tanaka maxHR, Karvonen, zones, VO₂max via HR-ratio, training effect,

`cardio_session.dart`, `cardio_audio_cues_service.dart`, `WorkoutTimerController`. Backend has

(mig 1965), `/vo2max/*` + `/cardio/*` endpoints, `recovery_signal_service.py` **already injected
into the generation prompt** (`generation_streaming.py:448`), and `workouts` interval columns
(mig 095: `is_interval`, `interval_type`, `work_seconds`, `rest_seconds`, `total_rounds`).

**Decisions (confirmed with user):** Full build · add VO₂max + HYROX exercises to the library now
(defer the other ~440) · VO₂max source = **native (Apple Watch/Garmin via HealthKit/Health
